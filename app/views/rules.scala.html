The Rules of Roulette

Basics

Roulette is a classic casino game where a small ball is thrown into a spinning wheel. Players bet on where the ball will land.

The Roulette Wheel

The European roulette wheel has 37 numbers from 0 to 36. The American variant has an additional double zero (00).

Betting Options

  • Single Numbers (Straight Up): Bet on a single number.
  • Red or Black: Bet on the color where the ball will land.
  • Even or Odd: Bet on whether the number will be even or odd.
  • High or Low: Bet on numbers 1-18 (low) or 19-36 (high).

Game Flow

  1. Players place their bets on the table.
  2. The croupier throws the ball into the spinning wheel.
  3. When the ball comes to rest, the winning number and color are announced.
  4. Winning bets are paid out, losing bets are collected.

Good luck and play responsibly!
